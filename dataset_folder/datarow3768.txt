Near-Earth Objects (NEOs) are comets and asteroids that have been nudged by the gravitational attraction of nearby planets into orbits that allow them to enter the Earth’s neighborhood. 🌎☄️  International efforts, led by NASA-sponsored search teams, have now discovered around 22,000 NEOs, and there are now more than 2,040 potentially hazardous asteroids known, whose orbits come close enough to Earth to warrant continued monitoring. 🛰️  Although rare, near-earth object impacts pose a significant and complex risk to both human life and critical infrastructure, and have the potential to cause unparalleled economic and environmental harm.
