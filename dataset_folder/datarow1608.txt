Descricão
###
Denis Levaillant is one of today's considerable French talents in music. He is recognized in particular for the composition of numerous musical shows, including the ballet "La petite danseuse" of the Opéra de Paris. He has the talent to synthesize in his art, genres usually considered to be antagonistic: composition/improvisation, classical / jazz, scholar / popular, acoustic / electro.
