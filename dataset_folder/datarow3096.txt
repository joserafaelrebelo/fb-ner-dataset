History rhymes and gunshots echo.  In light of the ongoing "operation" in Eastern Europe, we're taking a look back at the last time Russian/Soviet forces got stuck in the cold mud: The Winter War of 1939/40.  Who would you like to see portray the deadliest sniper of the 20th century? What stories of the more common ranks would you tell?
